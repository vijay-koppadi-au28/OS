SERVICES

IN-PATIENTS

KIMS is a multi-speciality hospital with 850 beds having vast and diverse facilities allotted for inpatient admissions health care needs. While you are our guest, we deliver only the best of our effort to make your stay comfortable. Our process of admission and discharge are very convenient and we have dedicated / designated staffs to look after your specific needs and assist you in completing every procedure with ease. Our staff will diligently explain and guide you through every step of admission or discharge.

OUT-PATIENTS

Health care needs have always been highly diverse and descriptive. The consultation in KIMS is through direct Walk-in, Referral, Designative / Repetitive for any existing General or Super-Specialty, we operate from 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM on all regular days, on Sundays and other Public Holidays all the consultations are available only through Emergency or Casualty.

DOCTOR(s) CONSULTATIONS

KIMS is well populated with a great and vibrant team of Specialists / Doctor(s) with vast clinical expertise and experience in their respective disciplines, and are available to deliver their services to the patients needs based on the requirements after the initial physician consultation for any further cross consultations.

BOOKINGS

Health is every individual concern in modern tiresome life style, so the needs of comprehensive services like health care checkups and diagnosis tests which are provisioned into a unique package are the current key points in Hospitals. KIMS provides with the best and competitive marked well defined Health Packages which are available with several benefits and comforts for Booking as per individual’s choice.
